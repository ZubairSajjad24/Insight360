USE CASE SUMMARY – Insight360 HR Intelligence System

Insight360 is a multi-agent HR analytics system designed to automate HR audits, discover risks and opportunities, and generate full executive reports with zero manual effort.

Key Capabilities:
- Automated HR audits using a structured dataset.
- Workflow-triggered multi-agent analysis.
- Identification of risks, opportunities, and workforce trends.
- Department-level breakdown for targeted decision-making.
- Fully formatted executive HR report.

Business Value:
Insight360 enables HR teams to streamline analysis, reduce reporting time, and gain data‑driven insights instantly. It improves decision-making, enhances employee wellbeing monitoring, and reveals operational inefficiencies across departments.

Target Users:
HR managers, People Analytics teams, Data-driven organizations.
