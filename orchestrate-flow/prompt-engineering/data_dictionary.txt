DATA DICTIONARY – HR Mock Dataset

employee_id: Unique identifier for each employee.
department: Employee department (HR, QA, Operations, Sales, etc.)
task_overdue_count: Number of overdue tasks assigned to the employee.
wellbeing_score: A score (1–10) representing employee wellbeing.
hours_worked: Average daily hours worked by the employee.
remote_attendance_hours: Logged hours for remote-day attendance.
overtime_hours: Number of overtime hours (beyond regular working hours).
job_level: Position level (1 = junior, 5 = senior).
gender: Male/Female/Other.
age: Employee age.
location: Employee’s working location.
