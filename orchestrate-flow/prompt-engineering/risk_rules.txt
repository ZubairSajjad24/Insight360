RISK IDENTIFICATION RULES

1. Task Overdue Risk:
   - High risk if task_overdue_count >= 3
   - Moderate risk if task_overdue_count = 2
   - Low risk if task_overdue_count <= 1

2. Wellbeing Risk:
   - High risk if wellbeing_score < 4
   - Moderate risk if wellbeing_score < 5
   - Good wellbeing if score >= 6

3. Overtime Risk:
   - High risk if overtime_hours > 3
   - Moderate risk if overtime_hours = 2–3
   - Low overtime if overtime_hours < 2

4. Attendance Risk:
   - Remote attendance hours = 0 → flag for missing logs
   - Repeated missing logs = high attendance risk

5. Department Risk:
   - Departments with wellbeing_score < 5 OR
     average overdue_count > 2 → red-flag department

OPPORTUNITY RULES

1. Low Task Overdue + High Wellbeing = Strong opportunity zone
2. Increasing average wellbeing over time = Positive trend
3. Departments with stable overtime < 2 = Efficiency opportunity

TREND RULES

1. Most employees working >9 hours/day → workload pressure trend
2. Wellbeing variation between departments > 2 points → inconsistency trend
3. Rising remote attendance gaps → monitoring trend
